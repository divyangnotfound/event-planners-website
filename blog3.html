Katie and Brendan’s Modern Summer Wedding

A summer wedding with blue details and touches of modern decor at The Bell Tower.

Catie and Brendan’s wedding celebration at The Bell Tower was filled with fresh blue accents, gold details, and a mix of classic and modern decor. The couple met at the University of Kentucky in Lexington through mutual friends at a football tailgate, bonding over their love for Kentucky basketball. Catie agreed to a group date with their friends— and the rest is history. Brendan proposed in Frankfurt at the Lovers Leap winery, surrounded by family and friends. To celebrate, the couple shared their first bottle of wine together!

When it came to the big day, Catie and Brendan knew they wanted to incorporate their alma mater’s colors of blue and white. We think it turned out beautifully! The wedding began with a beautiful ceremony in the main space, which was decorated with varying gold geometric pendant lights and greenery.

The ceremony arch was a modern gold rectangle with greenery and a touch of white flowers. After they were pronounced husband and wife, the celebration continued to our patio for cocktail hour. The space was decorated with light blue linens accented with white that tied into a green boxwood photo wall decorated with a white balloon arch and custom neon sign.

While guests mingled over cocktails and hors d'oeuvres, Hendecagon staff flipped the main space and mezzanine for the reception. Tables were set with crisp white linens, beautiful blue floral napkins, candles, and gold-framed table numbers. Some featured arrangements of greenery and white flowers in tall gold stands, while others were set with greenery surrounding the candles.
